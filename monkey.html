Monkey
Many people believe monkeys and primates are the same, however this is false. Monkeys are only a subspecies of primates. The main difference between monkeys and other primates is that monkeys have tails. Monkeys are generally smaller than other primates. The largest monkey is a mandril. Because monkeys are not as big and terrifying, their habitat is mostly taken over by humans. This means that there are many monkeys that are at risk of being endangered.
